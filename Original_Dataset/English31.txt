31) Beggar couple's fortune reduced to ashes
A Kalyan beggar couple in Mumbai had lakhs of rupees but now it has been reduced to ashes. Three sacks, containing currency notes, were gutted when the couple's hut caught fire on Wednesday. The incident became a talk of the town when the people came to know that the beggar couple had dumped the notes into the sacks kept inside the hut. Mohammad Rehman had collected the money by begging for last several years, says his wife Fatima.
